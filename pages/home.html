Our Integrated Software

Osdag

Open Steel Design and Graphics. A free, open-source, cross-platform tool for the analysis, design, and detailing of structural steel connections per IS 800:2007, with 3D visualization and CAD export. Developed under FOSSEE at IIT Bombay.

Website GitHub

osdagBridge

The bridge-design module of the Osdag ecosystem, built for modeling and detailing steel and composite steel-concrete bridge components — girders, diaphragms, stiffeners, and bearings — with automated design checks.

Website GitHub

3psLCCA

This site. A 3D IFC bridge model viewer paired with a Lifecycle Cost Analysis engine — browse the component catalog, inspect per-part specifications, and track long-term maintenance and material costs.

About Team OSDAG & Project

Our Mission

Team OSDAG builds free, open-source structural engineering software under the FOSSEE initiative at IIT Bombay — giving students and practicing engineers tools that are normally locked behind expensive proprietary licenses. Osdag started with steel connection design; osdagBridge extended that into steel and composite bridge components.

This site is where that work meets lifecycle costing: it parses IFC bridge models straight out of osdagBridge, renders every girder, pier, and diaphragm in 3D, and pairs each component with its specification and long-term cost data — so engineers can compare design alternatives on more than just upfront price.
